WHAT I DO

Hi! I'm an aspiring coder, artist, and code artist. My favorite area of math is geometry. My favorite flavor of coding is visualization.

Some of my work experiences include:

Motorola Solutions (summer 2015), where I wrote Perl scripts to test a wireless firmware download feature for two-way radios. I deconstructed binary data files that carried small chunks of the update, sent the update through a PC terminal emulator to the radio, and then parsed the radio's resulting log files to determine whether the download had been received correctly.

Art of Problem Solving Incorporated; I worked as an independent contractor to grade online school homework. Topics ranged from pre-algebra to calculus and Python and Java programming, and there was strong emphasis on problem-solving techniques as opposed to rote exercises one might find in a conventional textbook.
